# Privacy and AI Use

FollowUpPilot AI is a public portfolio demo and sales workflow assistant. It is designed for fictional, sample, or non-sensitive customer follow-up scenarios.

## User Data Guidance

Do not enter sensitive, confidential, regulated, or private information into the public demo.

Avoid entering:

- Real customer personally identifiable information
- Payment details
- Financing application details
- Medical, legal, or regulated data
- Internal company secrets
- Proprietary pricing, contracts, or confidential business records
- Passwords, API keys, or credentials

## AI Processing

When an OpenAI token is configured, selected user inputs may be sent to the AI provider to improve follow-up copy.

The app is built so that:

- Rules-based logic is the source of truth
- AI only improves wording, clarity, tone, and structure
- AI should not invent offers, deadlines, approvals, or promises
- AI failure falls back silently to rules-based output
- The app does not intentionally store user-submitted data

## Input Limits

The AI helper trims long prompts before AI enhancement to improve reliability and control cost.

Sensitive customer details should be generalized before being entered into the public demo.

## Public Demo Note

All built-in sample names, companies, and scenarios are fictional and created for portfolio demonstration purposes.
